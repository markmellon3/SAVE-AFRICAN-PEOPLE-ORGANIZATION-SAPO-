ABOUT US

Our Mission & Vision

We are excited to have you here! Our mission is to improve Wealth, Health, Education and to promote the Gospel and young talents of Africans. This is achieved through organizing different donation drives, providing free scholastic materials, distributing free food to people in need, and hosting various musical & gospel events.

Wealth

Supporting communities through donations and sustainable empowerment initiatives

Health

Providing essential food and nutrition to families and children in need

Education

Supplying books, pens, scholastic materials and school fees support

Gospel & Talents

Organizing gospel concerts and events to inspire and showcase young talents

Our Journey

2021

The project started in Kakindu Village, Katabi Town Council, Wakiso District. The very first step took place in a mining area where 25 people each received 2 kg of rice and 2 kg of sugar.

2022

We visited ST. Charles Primary School Namugonde and supported 50 pupils by giving them pens and books.

2023

A candidate from ST. Charles Primary School received term two school fees support.

2024

The SAPO team visited Kakindu Standard Primary School and provided pens and books to over 100 pupils.

Ongoing

We continue organizing donation series, free scholastic materials, food distribution, and gospel & musical events such as Cream and Kyomo Gospel Fest (27th December) to raise funds for equipment, books, pens, clothes, food, and more.
