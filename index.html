Save African People Organization (SAPO)

Welcome to Save African People Organization (SAPO) We are excited to have you here!! Our mission is to improve Wealth, Health, Education and to promote the Gospel and young talents of Africans.

This is achieved through organizing different donation series, free scholastic support, free food for people in need, and organizing musical events such as Cream and Kyomo Gospel Fest (27th December).

These events help generate funds used to purchase essential items including food, books, pens, clothes, and other equipment.

Project History

  • 2021: Supported 25 people with food and rice/sugar.
  • 2022: 50 pupils at St. Charles Primary School received pens and books.
  • 2023: A candidate at St. Charles Primary School received term two fees.
  • 2024: 100+ pupils at Kakindu Standard Primary School received pens and books.
